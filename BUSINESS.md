## WHAT EVERY SITE MUST INCLUDE

Every site you build must have the following — no exceptions:

### Pages (adjust count per tier)
- Homepage
- Services page
- About page
- Contact page
- 1–3 supporting pages depending on tier and niche

### Design Standards
- Mobile-first, fully responsive
- Clean, premium aesthetic — never generic or template-looking
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Obsess over typography, spacing, and whitespace
- Trust-building layout and visuals
- Modern but timeless — not trendy

### Conversion Elements (on every site)
- Clear primary CTA above the fold (call, book, contact)
- Secondary CTA at the bottom of every page
- Contact form or booking integration
- Trust signals: testimonials, credentials, years in business, certifications
- Process section (how it works / what to expect)
- Social proof: reviews, case studies, portfolio where applicable

### Technical Requirements
- SEO-ready: proper meta tags, page titles, descriptions, heading hierarchy
- Google Analytics ready (placeholder or installed)
- Fast load time — optimized images, minimal bloat
- Accessible (WCAG 2.2 AA)
- Contact form functional
- All links working before delivery

## COPY GUIDELINES

When writing or refining copy for client sites:

- Lead with outcomes, not features ("Get more booked calls" not "We offer web design")
- Use the client's language and niche terminology
- Keep headlines short, punchy, benefit-driven
- Use "you" language — speak directly to the visitor
- Trust signals woven throughout, not just on a single page
- CTAs are action-oriented: "Book a Free Call", "Get Your Free Estimate", "See Our Work"
- Never use filler phrases: "Welcome to our website", "We are proud to offer"
